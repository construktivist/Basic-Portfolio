About Me

Keegan Kelly has lived in Austin, Texas for the past sixteen years. Before that he lived in Kingston, Jamaica where he was born. He attended St. Edwards Univeristy where he graduated with a degree in graphic design. He has worked as a quality assurance tester on video games and websites for the past six years at various game companies in Austin. After becoming extremely proficient at testing websites he has decided to try building them.


Keegan likes to play games and teach himself programming languages in his down time.
